Here are some fascinating facts about the flying cicada

Click on the image to create a cicada!
  • Cicadas have three stages of life:
    1. Eggs: 6 weeks
    2. Nymphs: 13-17 years
    3. Adults:4-6 weeks
  • Female cicadas are silent, only the males make the mating calls
  • The sound of a male cicada can reach up to 100 decibels! That's louder than a tractor!

Below are some interesting facts about pigeons

  • The average sustained flight of a pigeon is 60mph!
  • Pigeons can tell what weather would be like, and might even be able to detect a volcanic eruption.
  • Pigeons are so intelligent that they can make sea rescues!
